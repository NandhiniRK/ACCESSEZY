What we offer

Everything in one calm space

No clutter, no overwhelm — just the tools that matter, laid out clearly for teachers and parents alike.

📊

Behavioural Analytics

Track observations over time with structured weekly logs. Spot patterns early with AI-powered wellbeing tags that flag academic overload, social stress, and emotional regulation.

🤖

AI Weekly Summaries

Automatically generated summaries of teacher notes give parents a clear, compassionate picture of their child's week — no jargon, just insight.

💡

Personalised Recommendations

Activity suggestions tailored to each child's current wellbeing profile, powered by semantic similarity — helping teachers and parents respond with intention.

📋

Homework & Materials

Assign homework and share learning resources directly through the platform. Parents see everything in one place, with due dates and links clearly visible.

💬

Community Support

A safe, moderated space for teachers and parents to share tips, ask questions, and support each other — with anonymous posting for sensitive topics.

🔒

Private & Structured

Role-based access ensures teachers manage student data, parents see only their child, and all information stays protected and appropriately scoped.
